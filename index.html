Five Largest Protests in U.S. History

The tradition of protests that demand progressive change in America continues.

Here are the country's five top protests in terms of attendance:

  1. George Floyd protests nationwide in 2020 with an estimated 26 million protesters.
  2. Women's March in 2017 drew about 5.6 million people nationwide.
  3. March for Our Lives, a student-led protest in 2018 against gun violence, drew about 2 million people./li>
  4. Women's March in 2018 drew about 1.5 million protesters.
  5. March on Washington for Lesbian, Gay and Bi Equal Rights and Liberation drew about 1 million people in 1993.
